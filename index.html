HOGWARTS

Hermione Granger burst into the wizarding world like a walking library with a wand attached. She is the kind of witch who will correct your pronunciation of “Wingardium Leviosa,” save your life with a spell she read about once, and then remind you there’s still homework due on Monday. Armed with overprepared lesson notes, an overstuffed book bag, and a moral compass that points due “fair and just,” Hermione turns sheer brainpower, stubborn courage, and frizzy hair into one of the most formidable combos Hogwarts has ever seen.

Ronald Weasley: strategic chess master, accidental hero, and part time collector of hand me down everything. Armed with a wonky wand, pockets full of crumbs, and more sarcasm than Galleons, he’s the friend who’ll complain the whole way into danger—and still be standing next to you when it really counts.

Ladies and gentlemen, please keep your wands, wallets, and souls inside the ride at all times – emerging from the shadows, fresh from a very intense nose-removal ritual, it’s the Dark Lord himself! He’s got seven backup copies of his soul, a snake for a best friend, and a résumé that’s basically just ‘terrorizing the wizarding world’ in increasingly dramatic fonts. Give a… cautious welcome to Lord Voldemort, proof that ‘too ambitious’ is, in fact, a thing.
