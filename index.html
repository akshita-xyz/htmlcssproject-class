Gryffindor

Welcome to Gryffindor, the house of the lion. Founded by Godric Gryffindor. Here live witches and wizards known for courage, daring, and a stubborn determination to do what is right, even when it is difficult. Our colours are scarlet and gold, our common room glows with firelight high in its tower, and our members stand together like family in the face of any challenge.

Hufflepuff

Welcome to Hufflepuff, founded by Helga Hufflepuff, where patience, kindness, and hard work are treasured above all. In this house, students value fairness, loyalty, and the quiet strength of those who keep their promises. Our colours are yellow and black, our emblem is the steadfast badger, and our cozy common room near the kitchens is a warm refuge where everyone has a place at the table.

Ravenclaw

Welcome to Ravenclaw, founded by Rowena Ravenclaw. the house for those who are endlessly curious and eager to learn. Here you will find students who delight in clever ideas, creative thinking, and solving the puzzles others overlook. Our colours are blue and bronze, our emblem is the soaring eagle, and our airy tower common room rewards wit and wisdom in equal measure.

Slytherin

Welcome to Slytherin, founded by Salazar Slytherin where ambition and determination are recognized as powerful virtues. This house gathers those who think strategically, value resourcefulness, and are willing to work relentlessly to achieve their goals. Our colours are green and silver, our emblem is the serpentine guardian of the deep, and our common room beneath the lake reflects the calm, focused mind of a born planner.
